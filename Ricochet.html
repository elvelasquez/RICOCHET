These are the instructions for Ricochet

  1. All circles can only move vertically or horizontally
  2. All crowns can only move diagonally
  3. Any piece can capture another, multiple captures in 1 move are possible
  4. After capturing, the attacking piece must be moved backwards along its attacking path
  5. You can ricochet backwards into an enemy and ricochet again into another
  6. The one with the most pieces in 10 whole turns wins
